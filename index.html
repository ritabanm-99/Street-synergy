Street Synergy


Street Synergy Community Association, Inc. is an organization of social change dedicated to maintaining and improving the integrity and strength of Buffalo's University District neighborhoods and improving the community's quality of life.

Our programs, "Better on Bailey" and "Urban Discovery" seek to strengthen the economic competitiveness of our community's business district and to increase homeownership, respectively. In addition to these long-term programs, our day to day activities revolve around keeping our neighborhoods clean and in good order.

Our Projects


Jazz Podcasts


Street Synergy is collaborating with the University at Buffalo to produce podcasts about the resurgence of Jazz in Buffalo, N.Y. in the decade from 1975-1985. The project is being produced by student interns from the Department of Media Studies and sponsored by the John H. Hunt Community Music Fund. Pilot podcast will be available in December 2022.
